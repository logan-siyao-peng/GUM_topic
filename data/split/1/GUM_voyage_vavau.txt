38	Climate
39	Vava'u has a tropical climate with average temperatures up to 29 º C in January and down to 24 º C in June .
40	It is sunny throughout the year .
41	Between November and April , it is more humid
42	and thunderstorms and cyclones do occur .
43	From May to September there are southeast trade winds
44	but during the summer months the winds are from the northeast .

48	Utu Vava'u .
49	This is the largest island ,
50	where Neiafu is found .
51	It is a limestone island with heights up to 213 m in the west .
52	The island provides a home to eleven indigenous bird species
53	and contains Mt. Talau national park , with some of the limited remaining native vegetation .
54	Tropical vegetation includes the pandanus or screw pine , the casuarina , and the mulberry tree ,
55	the bark of which is used
56	to make tapa cloth .
57	Pangaimotu .
58	This is the second largest island in the Group .
59	It can be seen across the water from Neiafu
60	and is connected by land bridge at the most southern point of ‘Utu Vava’u .
61	There are some good beaches and plenty of secluded cove beaches and protected bays with good snorkelling .
62	Avai'o'vuna Swamp is a small coastal wetland on the island .
63	Hunga Island .
64	This is some 35 minutes from Neiafu Harbour
